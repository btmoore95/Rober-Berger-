Robert Berger

Robert Berger Associates is a strategy and branding consultancy primarily focused on the non-profit sector. We have successfully completed engagements in strategic planning, collaboration planning and workshop facilitation.

Robert has extensive experience in strategic planning, brand management and market research built during a 30+ year career as a corporate executive and as a consultant. He is also a trained facilitator with an MBA from the University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business.

Robert works alone or engages partners to add capabilities as necessary.
